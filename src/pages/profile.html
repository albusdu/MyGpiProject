KYC Verification

KYC or Know Your Customer is a mandatory process required by international and local regulations to identify and verify the identity of our clients. Please provide the listed documents to have full access to our features and offers.

Necessary documents

Proof of Identity

Proof of Identity: Passport, Driving License or official Government issued ID card.

  • • Must be a Passport, Driving License or other official Government issued ID card
  • • Both Front and Back of ID card must be received
  • • Document must be in date and not expired
  • • Document must be showing expiry date
  • • Document must show your unaltered photo
  • • Document must show your date of birth
  • • Water marks on documents must be visible
  • • Document must be with colors
Proof of Address

Proof of Address: Utility bill, TV bill, Tax bill or bank statement displaying your name and address in full

  • • Must be either a utility bill, TV bill, Tax Bill or bank statement (copy of paper statement)
  • • Must show your FULL address including any post/ zip codes
  • • Must show your FULL name
  • • Must display official logo of issuing company
  • • Must be dated within the last 90 days
  • • Must be able to see full document
Proof of Deposit

Proof of Deposit: Photo of credit card, Skrill/Neteller page, etc.

You will also have to submit documents to verify your Deposit and/or Withdrawal methods depending on the chosen method. Please check below information on the methods you have used to find out what you need to send:
  • • If using Neteller or Skrill: Photo of profile page on Neteller or Skrill showing profile with postal address visible.
  • • If using Credit Card: Front and Back of card used to deposit, with middle 6 numbers and CVC/CVV covered The first 6 and last 4 digits must be visible Must correspond with credit card number used on casino
  • • Picture must show whole card
Note : Screenshots are not accepted.
