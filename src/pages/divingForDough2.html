What is REEF OF REWARDS?

REEF OF REWARDS is Yggdrassil’s global campaign that runs as follows: 10 May 2021 00:01CEST –16 May 2021 23:59CEST.

How to participate in the promotion?

· REEF OF REWARDS is open to all customers who are aged 18 years or over and have registered an account at Betvili.com

· There is no minimum bet required to participate in any of the stages of the REEF OF REWARDS campaign.

· Only bets made in play for real mode qualify for the campaign.

· To participate in the campaign, players shall confirm that they wish to take part in any of the stages of the campaign by clicking “join now” in the pop-up window. Participating players will take part in the campaign by placing a bet in the participating games during the campaign period. No additional payment is required to take part in the campaign.

Which Slots participate in the promotions?

Atlantean Gigarise, Valley of the Gods, Multifly!, Hades.

How does it work?

- To complete the mission, all objectives have to be fulfilled accordingly in each game:

- Atlantean Gigarise – Land 60 wild symbols in base game

- Valley of the Gods - Activate statues 3 times

- Multifly! –In base game, reach 3rd dropdown 3 times

- Hades – Trigger fight feature in Base game 2 times

- All players who complete the mission for the first time will be provided with a unique player number

- The unique player number will be highlighted for the players on the leaderboard in the game client to follow their position. Players who are not qualified for the leaderboard will not have their position displayed

- The number of spins made to complete the mission determines the player’s position on the leaderboard. The player who completes the mission in the least number of rounds will finish at position 1 and win the 1st prize

- If there are two or more players who complete the mission with the same number of rounds, the player completing it first will have higher position on the leaderboard

- Player’s position is refreshed every 60 seconds

- Players’ position on the leaderboard can change throughout the mission

- Mission progress is automatically restarted when players can no longer improve the score. The best score will always be kept

- Mission progress can be restarted manually by the player after 25 spins. Restart button can be found in the game client

- Prizes will be credited as cash

- Players can win more than 1 random prize reward during the campaign
