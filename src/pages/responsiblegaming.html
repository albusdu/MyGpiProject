Responsible gaming

At Betvili, we aspire to provide you with a safe, secure, regulated environment to ensure a pleasant and rewarding experience. We encourage our clients to use our services responsibly. Casino games have been a source of entertainment for hundreds of years. We encourage our clients to enjoy their gaming experience as much as possible without affecting their daily lives. We strongly encourage that the money they spend is set aside for leisure and not for other purposes. As a gaming company, we cannot guarantee that you will win; thus, we would like to encourage our customers not to rely on possible earnings from gambling.

While most customers play responsibly, we understand that a small minority of customers can develop gambling problems.

At Betvili, the well-being of our customers is vital. We understand our role within responsible gambling and recommend that you:

• Treat your betting as a form of entertainment and only use money you have set aside for your leisure. • Do not bet or wager amounts larger than you are comfortable with. • Do not bet or wager with money you cannot afford to lose. • Do not chase your losses. • Do not let your gaming take up more time than you were willing to spend on this activity.

If you have any concerns about your gambling habits and feel are no longer able to follow the recommendations mentioned above, we strongly recommend that you seek advice from accredited organizations that offer support and advice, such as

GamCare www.gamcare.org.uk Keep It Fun https://keepitfun.rank.com/ Gambling Therapy https://www.gamblingtherapy.org/en

Moreover, you can contact our Betvili support agents at compliance@betvili.com to discuss our self-exclusion process.

Sometimes taking a break from gaming can help you gather your thoughts and start making more rational and reasonable wagering decisions.

Upon request, we will place your account on a minimum of a 24-hour cooling-off period. This will be followed by one of our support agents informing you of the voluntary 1-week, 1-month, 6-months, or permanent self-exclusion available.

Please take note that accounts closed under our self-exclusion policy cannot be reversed or reopened for any reason. However, once your self-exclusion has expired, we will email you to let you know your account is active again. Permanent self-exclusion will remain in place indefinitely.

Gambling under the age of 18 is categorically prohibited by our policies. If you have any minors living in your household, we urge you to review the following parental control software links regarding control and restrictions of the content accessible on your devices.
