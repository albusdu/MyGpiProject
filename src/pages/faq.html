How can I make a screenshot?

A screenshot is an image that shows the current contents on your computer. After taking a screenshot, the image will be saved in your computer as a file, and can easily be attached to e-mails to send to our support team. To take a screenshot in Windows, just follow the steps outlined below: Step 1: Ensure that your screen's current view is the image you want to take a screenshot of. Close or minimize any additional windows that may be obscuring the view. Press the 'Print Screen' button on your keyboard. This button is usually located on the right-hand side of the keyboard. Open a 'Paint' or 'Word' program on your computer and then press Ctrl + V to paste the image in. You can also click the 'Paste' option from the 'Edit' menu to do this. You will then see your print screen image appear in the document. Save this file to your computer using a name that will help you quickly identify it. We also recommend that you save it to your desktop to allow faster access to the file. Attach this file into an e-mail addressed to support@betvili.com, including all the additional information about the error. To take a screenshot on a Mac, just follow the steps outlined below: Ensure that the current view on your screen is the image you want to take a screenshot of. Close or minimize any additional windows that may be obscuring the view. Press Shift + CMD + 4, then click on the spacebar to make the camera appear. Click the mouse to take a snapshot of the current window open on your screen. This image will automatically save to your computer desktop, with the present time and date. Attach this file into an e-mail addressed to support@betvili.com, including all the additional information about the error.
