About The Jetx

WHAT IS JETX?

jetx is an innovative game that allows players can win up to 1,000x of their bet. the winning formula is simple: bet amount x the multiplier = win

HOW TO PLAY?

players can make a bet before each takeoff. the longer the aircraft flies in the air the bigger the multiplier. players must cash out before the aircraft crashes to claim their winnings. if the aircraft crashes then the bet is lost.

players can also use the auto-cashout feature. for example, if a player sets a cashout at 3.0x, if the multiplier reaches 3.01x, your winnings will be automatically cashed out and you will win 3x of your initial bet.
