What is Easter Escape?

- Easter Escape is Yggdrassil’s global campaign that runs as follows: 01 April 2021 10:00CEST –11 April 2021 23:00CEST

How to participate in the promotion?

· Easter Escape is open to all customers who are aged 18 years or over and have registered an account at Betvili.com

· There is no minimum bet required to participate in any of the stages of the Easter Escape campaign.

· Only bets made in play for real mode qualify for the campaign.

· To participate in the campaign, players shall confirm that they wish to take part in any of the stages of the campaign by clicking “join now” in the pop-up window. Participating players will take part in the campaign by placing a bet in the participating games during the campaign period. No additional payment is required to take part in the campaign.

How does it work?

· Any bet placed on the slot/s Easter Island, Easter Island 2, Multifly, Golden Fishtank, Holmes, Jackpot Raiders, Reel Desire during the campaign period can trigger a random mystery prize from the prize pool.

· Each bet can win only 1 random prize reward.

· Prizes will be credited as cash.

· Players can win more than 1 random prize reward during the campaign.
