Terms And Conditions

What is Booongoo’s Bday?

Booongoo’s Bday is a series of Booongoo’s tournaments. From 06.05.2021 0:00 UTC to 25.05.2021 23:59 UTC, players will collect points towards the Daily Regular Leaderboards & the Series Leaderboard. The promotion is divided into 3weeks—each week ending with a Series Leaderboard.

Split as: 06.05.2021 0:00 UTCto11.05.2021 23:59 UTC(week1) 13.05.2021 0:00 UTCto18.05.2021 23:59 UTC(week2) 20.05.2021 0:00 UTCto25.05.2021 23:59 UTC(week3)

The winners of Regular Leaderboards will win a share of 5,000 EUR each day The winners of the Series Leaderboard will win a share of 10,000 EUR

What are the prizes?

Grand Total Prize Pool is 120,000 EUR for this Campaign combined from several pools by the following structure:

Week 1 - 5,000 EUR for each of the 6 Regular Tournaments + 10,000 EUR for the Series Leaderboard Week 2 - 5,000 EUR for each of the 6 Regular Tournaments + 10,000 EUR for the Series Leaderboard Week 3 - 5,000 EUR for each of the 6 Regular Tournaments + 10,000 EUR for the Series Leaderboard

How to participate in the promotion?

To participate in the promotion, you must play the following slots from April 1 through April 21

To be qualified and have a right to claim the prize player should fulfill the following conditions:

The minimum Bet is 1 EUR The minimum number of spins is 100

All points collected during the qualification stage are saved and considered for a player after passing such qualification. Non-qualified players are also shown on the Leaderboard but stripped in grey until they are qualified.

How are the points calculated?

The points will be calculated in two different ways: HSRM (Highest Single Round Multiplier) - the highest win-to-bet ratio/multiplier is considered Example: Bet = 5 EUR and Win= 150 EUR, Win-to-bet multiplier = 150/5 The player gets 30 points until a higher multiplier occurs.

HSRP (Highest Single Round Payout) - the biggest payout in money equal to the number of assigned points Example: Bet = 2 EUR and Win = 340 EUR The player gets 340 points until a higher payout happens.
