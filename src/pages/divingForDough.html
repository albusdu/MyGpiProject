What is POSEIDON’S BOOTY?

POSEIDON’S BOOTY is Yggdrassil’s global campaign that runs as follows: POSEIDON’S BOOTY Prize Drop: 03 May 2021 10:00CEST - 09 May 2021 23:00CEST

How to participate in the promotion?

· POSEIDON’S BOOTY is open to all customers who are aged 18 years or over and have registered an account at Betvili.com

· There is no minimum bet required to participate in any of the stages of the POSEIDON’S BOOTY campaign.

· Only bets made in play for real mode qualify for the campaign.

· To participate in the campaign, players shall confirm that they wish to take part in any of the stages of the campaign by clicking “join now” in the pop-up window. Participating players will take part in the campaign by placing a bet in the participating games during the campaign period. No additional payment is required to take part in the campaign.

Which Slots participate in the promotions?

Atlantean Gigarise, Holmes and the Stolen Stones, Jackpot Raiders, Valley of the Gods 2, Lucky Neko, Dwarf Mine, Vault of Fortune, Robin -Sherwood Marauders

How does it work?

· Any bet placed on the slot/s Atlantean Gigarise, Holmes and the Stolen Stones, Jackpot Raiders, Valley of the Gods 2, Lucky Neko, Dwarf Mine, Vault of Fortune, Robin -Sherwood Marauders during the campaign period can trigger a random mystery prize from the prize pool.

· Each bet can win only 1 random prize reward.

· Prizes will be credited as cash.

· Players can win more than 1 random prize reward during the campaign.
