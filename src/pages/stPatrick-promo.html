About The Promo

St.Patrick Daily GreenWards? St.Patrick Daily GreenWards is a special promotion by Booongoo. From 04.03.2021 0:00 UTC and 23.03.2021 23:59 UTC, players will be able to complete daily goals and receive corresponding rewards for doing so. Daily Rewards campaign runs in cycles of up to 5 calendar days each (always using the UTC +0 timezone) with the ability to compete the Daily Goal up to 3 times.
