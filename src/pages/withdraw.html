How to make a withdrawal?

To make a withdrawal simply go to the "Balance" section of your account, and press the “Withdraw” button.

What are the rules for withdrawing funds from my balance?

All withdrawals must be processed through the same payment method chosen by you when placing a deposit, unless we decide otherwise or are unable to do so. Suppose you deposit using a number of payment methods. In that case, we reserve the right to split your withdrawal across such payment methods and process each part through the respective payment method at our discretion and per anti-money laundering policies and regulation. Please visit the Payment Methods page for detailed information and find what Payment Methods you may use for withdrawing funds.

Is there a maximum withdrawal rule I should know about?

Please, visit the Payment Methods page for detailed information on available payment methods and their respective withdrawal limits.

How long after I request a withdrawal will I get my money?

Generally, we process our withdrawal requests instantly. Withdrawal time might vary depending on the payment method used. If you choose to withdraw funds to a credit card, your request will be reviewed, and the funds will be transferred within the same business day. If you choose to withdraw funds through an alternative payment method, you will get instructions on how to proceed and how long the transfer will take. For more information, please visit our Payments page.

Can I cancel a withdrawal request?

Yes, you can cancel your withdrawal request from your account, before it's approved by us
