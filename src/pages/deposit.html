What is CV2?

A CV2 number is the last three digits on the signature strip on the back of your card. You will need to enter this whenever you make a deposit, on the site, otherwise, the deposit will not be successful. It's also known as the Card Security Code (CSC), CVC or CVV.

What is 3D secure?

3D secure is a security measure that is specific to your bank. It means that you have to enter a password when you deposit funds online. This password is only known to you and your bank. Betvili has no control over this section of the deposit processand if you have any issues with the passwords at this stage, please use the Help options presented to you by your bank.
